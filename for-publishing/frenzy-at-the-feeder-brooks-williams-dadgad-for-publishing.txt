
Frenzy at the Feeder by Brooks Williams
Based mostly on YouTube lesson: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ahpz-Mqihok

   
DADGAD  (from EADGBe, downtune the low E and high B and e strings by 2 notes each)
no capo
Time signature: 2/4
All notes are sequenced to precise timings except where I make a comment
Fingering style is called 'travis picking' where the right hand 
  thumb plays the alternating low notes on the beat and the rest of the 
  right hand / strumming hand fingers pluck the melody.
Feel free to modify and improve under a copyleft license and contact me
on my website taoketao.github.io if there's a problem (especially you,
Brooks Williams). Enjoy! -DJ Plobs / morgan ~ morgan
