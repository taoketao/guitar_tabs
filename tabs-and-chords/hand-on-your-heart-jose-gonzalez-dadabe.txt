 |------------------------------------------------------------------|
  
 Tuning: D-A-D-A-B-E  This is the same tuning used in "Cycling
                      Trivialities." If you don't want to tune the G
                      string up a whole step, you can tune either a
                      half step down (Db-Ab-Db-Ab-Bb-Eb) or a whole
                      step down (C-G-C-G-A-D) and capo accordingly.
  
 Time Signature: 4/4, with a 3-3-2 eighth-note accent pattern (It's
                 more like 2/2, but 4/4 is easier to count.)
  
 Key Signature: D Major (F#, C#)
  
 Form: Introduction - Verse 1 - Verse 2 - Chorus - Verse 3 - Chorus - Coda
