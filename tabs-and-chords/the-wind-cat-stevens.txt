This is my version of "The Wind" by Cat Stevens.  In my opinion, its a
fantastically lovely short song that always makes me smile.
 
[Intro:] Just the D chord with an A on the high e string.
 
e|--------5-----------5-----------5-----------5----------------------------|
B|--3-----------3-----------3-----------3----------------------------------|
G|-----2-----2-----2-----2-----2-----2-----2-----2-------------------------|
D|--0-----------0-----------0-----------0----------------------------------|
A|-------------------------------------------------------------------------|
E|-------------------------------------------------------------------------|
 
This is repeated for most of the song with slight variations.
To lead into the chorus is a simple variation on the A chord. Instead of a hammer-on
2 to 3 its a pull off from 2 to 0 on the B string.  This is
noted in tab in the far right section of the "verse"
