    V ^ V ^   ^ v A   ^ V ^   ^ V ^

I use my left hand thumb to fret the low strings in figure A, but
if your fingers are bigger it's probably more comfortable to fret 
both of the two low strings with left hand middle finger.

Variations: 
 - For every repeated time you play the line, on the very first beat 
   slide from the 220100 /// 4403xx instead of the hammer on:
      |    |     
  0*0-|-------
  2-0-|-----0-
  3-1-|-1/3-4-
  0-0-|-0---5-
  2-2-|-2/4-4-
  2-2-|-2/4-4-
  V ^   V   V 

 - Every fourth bar, strum the last note twice quickly. Instead of a 
   '^', do 'v^'. You can also omit the upstroke; getting the downstroke
   is the distinctive, important sound.
