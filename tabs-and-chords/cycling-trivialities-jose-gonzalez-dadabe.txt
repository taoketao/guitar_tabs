--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                    CYCLING TRIVIALITIES - Jose Gonzalez
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Tabbed by: Tom Walter (with help from jamminjohn)
Email: walter dot tom at gmail dot com
 
Tuning: DADABE (Capo 2)
 
Ignore the tuning listed at the top of the page on Ultimate Guitar, they don't have an
option for this tuning, Drop D is the closest they have but note the G string is tuned UP
a tone to A. If you don't want to tune your G string up a tone, you can use CGCGAD, Capo 4.
 
Played with triplets all the way through (as noted on 1st two bars)
 
The [1. [2. etc are alternate endings for the sections indicated.
 
The fingerpicking follows the same pattern almost throughout, with only a couple of
spots that it has to change slightly. The pattern is:
 
Thumb, index, middle, ring, index, middle.
 
It goes a bit tricky in the bridge, you'll have to figure that out.
 
From the part labelled middle 16 until the end, the pattern is much the same but you
just use your thumb for the lower notes that play at the same time as the high notes
on the ring finger, ie
 
Thumb, index, middle, thumb/ring, index, middle.
 
Thanks to jamminjohn for suggesting the alternate tuning which made a lot of
parts fit better. And the many other commenters for pointing out that it should really
be tuned a tone higher to be authentic.
