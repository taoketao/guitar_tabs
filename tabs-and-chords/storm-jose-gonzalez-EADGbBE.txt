There's no real verse/chorus structure to this song, so I've simply broken it
into the main parts. Parts A, B, and C are fairly similar but they each have
unique variations. They should be played in this order:
 
  Intro
  Part A, no singing
  Part B, "Send me your position" ...
  Part B, "Why do you keep on running" ...
  Part C, no singing
  Part B, "As soon as the storm is over" ...
  Part C, "Oooooh, as soon as the storm is over" ...
  Outro, "As sooon"...
